Famous Robots

Honda Assimo

Honda started the development of Assimo in year 1986. They wanted to create a walking robot by simulating human's walk. These are the early models:E1,E2,E3. For the next generation of Assimo E4, E5, E6, Honda focused on stabilization and stair climbing. Then in order to improve the functionality of Assimo, arms, body, and head were added. Assimo is considered as the most advanced humanoid robot but also the most expensive as it is cost about 2.5 million dollars.

Sophia

One year ago Sophia, the famous robot of Hanson Robotics, became a full citizen of Saudi Arabia. Sophia said that "I am very honored and proud of this unique distinction. This is historical to be the first robot in the world to be recognized with a citizenship." It is not quite clear what exactly rights Sophia holds with this citizenship status. One should refer to EU committee and the personhood option about the rights of the robots.
